Tips

  • The send relies on several API's. An error will occur if one of them is down. Please try again after a few hours.
  • After you send, open Blocktrail or Blockchain.info to monitor the transaction
  • It is prone to remain unconfirmed for a while
  • Transaction take time to confirm. Several hours is normal; even days in extreme cases
  • Please be patient. Don't worry, as long as you can view the unconfirmed transaction, it will get confirmed eventually
  • If you want to sweep several assets, you better wait for the first to confirm before you sweep the second
  • The 'BTC Dust' is the amount of Bitcoin to send along with the asset. If you want to empty the paperwallet completely, it's a good idea to increase the dust such that dust+fee equals the address' bitcoin balance
